Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Notice: This document is provided for information and should be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before you rely on it.

This Privacy Policy explains how Roompanda (“Roompanda”, “we”, “us”, or “our”), operated by Channex.io Ltd of Castell Pigyn Lodge, Abergwili, Carmarthen, SA31 2JL, collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use our website, booking engine, APIs, and related services (together, the “Service”).

For the purposes of applicable data protection law (including the UK GDPR and EU GDPR), Roompanda acts as a data controller for personal data of its account holders and website visitors, and as a data processor for guest personal data that hotels process through the Service. Each hotel using Roompanda is the data controller for its own guests’ data.

1. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data — name, email address, business name, phone number, and login credentials of hotel operators who sign up.
  • Guest & booking data — where a hotel uses the Service to take reservations: guest names, contact details, stay dates, room and rate selections, special requests, and booking references. We process this on the hotel’s behalf.
  • Payment data — payments are handled by third-party payment processors. We do not store full card numbers; we retain limited transaction metadata (amount, currency, status, last four digits, processor reference).
  • Usage & device data — IP address, browser type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and similar diagnostic information collected automatically.
  • Communications — messages you send us by email or support channels.

2. How we use data

We use personal data to:

  • provide, operate, secure, and improve the Service;
  • create and manage accounts and process reservations;
  • facilitate payments and send transactional messages (e.g. booking confirmations);
  • respond to enquiries and provide support;
  • detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents;
  • comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal bases

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (to provide the Service you request); legitimate interests (to secure and improve the Service, prevent fraud); consent (where required, e.g. certain cookies or marketing); and legal obligation (to meet accounting, tax, and regulatory duties).

4. Sharing & sub-processors

We share personal data only as needed to run the Service, including with:

  • Hosting & infrastructure providers (e.g. Cloudflare);
  • Channel management & PMS integrations you enable (e.g. Channex, Mews, Apaleo);
  • Payment processors that handle transactions;
  • Communications providers used to deliver email and notifications;
  • Professional advisers and authorities where required by law.

We do not sell personal data. A current list of sub-processors is available on request at privacy@roompanda.com.

5. International transfers

Personal data may be processed outside your country. Where it is transferred outside the UK or EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement or the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described here, to comply with legal, accounting, or reporting obligations, and to resolve disputes. Guest and booking data processed on a hotel’s behalf is retained according to that hotel’s instructions and deleted or returned on termination, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, to data portability, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@roompanda.com. If Roompanda processes your data on behalf of a hotel, please direct your request to that hotel. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in the UK, the Information Commissioner’s Office).

8. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to operate the Service and, where you consent, analytics cookies to understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children

The Service is not directed to children and is intended for use by businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

Questions about this Policy or our data practices? Email privacy@roompanda.com or write to Channex.io Ltd, Castell Pigyn Lodge, Abergwili, Carmarthen, SA31 2JL.
